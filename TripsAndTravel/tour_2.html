Island Beach Holidays BORACAY The Island Paradise

Day 01 Arrival MANILA or CEBU

Arrival Manila. Welcome reception and airport assistance. Transfer to hotel for accommodations. Tour briefing.

If flying in via Cebu:

One time entrance ticket to Crown Regency Tower's Sky Adventure and Edge coaster Experience

Day 02 MANILA or CEBU/BORACAY

If flying in via Manila:

AM - Choice of Hop On/Hop Off Manila tour by land or by Pasig river cruise Transfer to airport to take early afternoon flight for Kalibo.

If flying in via Cebu

Transfer out to Mactan domestic airport for flight to Kalibo Transfer to Caticlan Jetty port to board motorized boat for Boracay Island. Check in at resort for accommodations. Rest of afternoon is free. (B)
