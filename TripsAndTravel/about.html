About Us

Company Profile

We at T.R.I.P.S (Travel Related Incentive Programmes and Services, Inc.), are proud to be one of the leading tour operator in the Philippines specializing in Inbound operations. Established on February 1996 by two fine gentlemen who dreamt of making a difference in delivering tour services, the Company has been incorporated with a team of experts whose involvement in the industry covers almost 25 years.

"This company will think, eat and breathe quality. Quality as defined by our clients", a vision which our company will always keep in mind. With our nine years in the industry, we were able to established and maintained good working relationship with industry partners, suppliers and other related entities which made us competent enough to deliver high-quality services.
