emergency lightning

Once all fire alarms go off and right before fire suppression systems are in full effect, people need to get out of harm's way and evacuate the building in a safe and orderly way. Don't overlook getting an emergency lighting system, which will direct co-workers and employees to the nearest fire exit.

Like all emergency equipment, lights are also important during times of crises as they will help lead the way to safety. At Firestop, we hope to also lead you to a secure and safe work or home environment.

We offer a variety of emergency lighting systems and offer installation, inspection and maintenance of lighting bulbs and other hardware components to make sure that lights will work during an emergency.

Speak to any of our Firestop customer or sales representatives for more information on emergency lighting and other fire protection systems and services that we offer.
