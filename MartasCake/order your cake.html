How Would You Like to Order

method #1
Thur Our Website?

Simply fill up and submit our Online Order Form.   Expect a confirmation of your order details via email.

method #2
By Email?

Select a couple of cake designs from our Gallery.   Attach cake design photos (or their album & photo names/numbers), indicate preferred cake size/s, and send to any of our branch email addresses.   If you already have a photo of your desired cake design, attach it (as jpeg), indicate preferred cake size/s and send to any of our branch email addresses.

method #3
By Phone?

Call any of our branches, ask for any Account Executive and discuss your order.   Expect a Job Order upon your confirmation via email.

method #4
Thru One of Our Branches?

Visit a branch.   View albums, see sample sizing and discuss with our Account Executives.   Expect a Job Order upon your confirmation via email.
