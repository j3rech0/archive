St. Luke's Medical Center Quezon City is easily accessible from the city's main artery, EDSA.

FROM THE NORTH◄

On EDSA, proceed southbound, past SM North EDSA, and the Quezon Avenue Flyover.

Before the Kamias-Kamuning Flyover, turn right on Timog Avenue, at the corner of GMA-7 and EDSA.

Proceed on Timog Avenue until the roundabout with Boy Scout statues, and turn left to Tomas Morato.

Go straight on Tomas Morato until you encounter a “T” intersection, and turn right on E. Rodriguez Sr. Avenue.

Proceed on E. Rodriguez Sr. Avenue, past the Quezon City Sports Club and St. Joseph’s College on your right.

The hospital is right after St. Joseph’s College.
