OVERVIEW


Allure Models International was initially set up in Singapore and subsequently in the Philippines by Ms. Tasha Teo ("Coach Tasha") and her immediate goals were to establish a training academy where models and professionals can learn and improve themselves through various forms of coaching classes, consultancy services and training sessions.

Being a former international model herself, Coach Tasha wishes to create a community of models and professionals who will exude a high level of self-confidence and deportment and bear the undeniable trademark of being part of Allure Models International.

MISSION STATEMENT

Allure Models International envisions being the premiere one-stop professional modeling consultancy and management firm, regionally and globally. It aims to impart the requisite experience and knowledge, not only to models, but also to professionals alike, in order to substantially boost their self-image and confidence levels to greater heights.
