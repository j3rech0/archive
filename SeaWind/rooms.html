Rooms info




Contemporary luxury and classic design is our commitment to every jest room we offer. From our Bahay Kubo to the Spanish bah na bat. Sea Wind features several truly Filipino architectural styles that complements their verdant surroundings. Within the rooms the elegance of traditional Filipino pieces are integrated with the creature of a luxury hotel.

Sprawling over a hectare of this tropical paradise the structures are clustered and sprawled over the property to maintain as much of its natural environment as possible and various slices of Filipino life.
